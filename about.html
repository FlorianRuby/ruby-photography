ABOUT

I'm Florian, a sixteen-year-old photographer based in Switzerland. I started taking pictures when I was twelve because my mother did, so naturally I wanted to take some for myself too. From that point onward photography has been a hobby of mine.

Since then, I've always tried to photograph everything, whether it was my family, places I've visited, cars, or just random scenes I found myself in. Over the years, I have been able to work with clients and helped them realize their visions.

During this time, I've gained experience and skills not just in photography but also in editing videos and photos, where I also developed an interest for graphic design. Now I want to use my skills to help people capture the pictures they envision and collaborate with more clients and people I look up to.
