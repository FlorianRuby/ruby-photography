PORTFOLIO
Ever since starting my photography journey over four years ago during Covid, I've proudly built up my portfolio with a wide variety of photographic genres.

My journey began with macro photography, introduced to me by my mother when I was twelve.

Since then, I have explored landscape photography, capturing the beauty of the places I've visited. I have also ventured into cloudscape photography, which I particularly love as clouds are visible to everyone, yet each person sees them differently, and no cloud is ever seen twice.

Over the years, I have delved into architectural photography and had the opportunity to shoot clients' cars. My interest in aviation has also led me to photograph planes.

Recently, I have developed an interest in street photography, as I love capturing everyday moments that tell their own personal stories.
